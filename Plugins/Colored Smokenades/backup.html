Colored Smokenades
By xPaw

.: Description :.
This plugin makes smokegrenade an color grenade Requested here.
.: Video :.
http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lG1etxctW5U&fmt=18
.: Cvars :.
sv_colored_smoke 0-2 (default: 1)
  • 0 - Colored smokenades disabled
  • 1 - Random type from 6 colors
  • 2 - Team type, the smoke will have color of team who threw that smoke

[IMG]http://img155.**************/img155/290/61455877.th.jpg[/IMG] [IMG]http://img268.**************/img268/1839/57605632.th.jpg[/IMG]
.: Known bugs :.
  • Smoke not go out on new round.
